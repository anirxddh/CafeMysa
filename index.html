About Cafe Mysa

We are a cafe that serves the best continental food in town along with freshly brewed coffee. Our target crowd is mostly the young generation, from age 14 to 45. We particularly attract those in their twenties (21-30 age group) including students, couples, entrepreneurs, employed professionals, and gym enthusiasts.

At Cafe Mysa, we believe that great food and exceptional coffee can transform ordinary moments into extraordinary memories. Every dish is crafted with care, every cup is brewed to perfection, and every guest is treated like family.
